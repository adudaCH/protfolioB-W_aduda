'American Sniper'

American Sniper (2014) is a biographical war drama based on the life of Chris Kyle, a Navy SEAL sniper whose pinpoint accuracy made him a legend during the Iraq War. Directed by Clint Eastwood, the film follows Kyle through four tours of duty, where he earns the nickname “Legend” for protecting countless American lives. While he excels in combat, Kyle struggles to adjust to life back home with his wife and children, haunted by the trauma and weight of his experiences. The story explores themes of duty, sacrifice, and the psychological toll of war on soldiers and their families.
