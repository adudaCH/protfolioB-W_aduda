HTML project

'Coffee'

Who doesn't like a free fresh morning coffee? This project contains a simple HTML page designed as a landing page for a coffee promotion, where users can submit their details to receive a free coffee coupon. The page is crafted to be inviting and straightforward, allowing users to quickly and easily enter their information, such as their name and email address, to claim their coupon. The design is minimalist yet effective, using simple HTML and CSS technologies to create a clean and responsive layout that looks great on any device. The form is central to the page, with a prominent call-to-action that encourages users to take advantage of the offer. This project is perfect for a coffee shop or a brand looking to engage customers with a small, delightful incentive, while also collecting valuable customer information for future marketing efforts. With a focus on simplicity and functionality, the page provides an effortless user experience, making it easy for anyone to get their hands on a free coffee and start their day right.
